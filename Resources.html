Meet our guest authors!

Bob Shea

Bob Shea is an award winning author and illustrator of picture books. His books include Chez Bob, the Dinosaur vs… series, and Unicorn thinks he’s pretty great. In addition to writing books, he has worked with Nick Jr., Playhouse Disney, and Noggin. He also designed the famous green-head PBS logo.

Learn more about Bob at www.bobshea.com!

Mo Willems

Mo Willems is the award winning author and illustrator of over 70 children’s books. He is the creator of beloved picture book series like the Pigeon, Elephant and Piggie, Snuffle Bunny, and Unlimited Squirrels. Don’t Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus! was the inaugural induction into the Picture Book Hall of Fame. Willems earned 11 Emmy nominations and 6 Emmy Awards for his work on Sesame Street.

Learn more about Mo at www.hiddenpigeoncompany.com!

Dav Pilkey

Dav Pilkey is the award winning author and illustrator of Captain Underpants, Dog Man, Kat Kong, and the Dumb Bunnies. Growing up, Dad had difficulty in school due to ADHD and dyslexia, causing him to turn to drawing. His love for comic books has grown into a global success with his titles selling in multiple languages worldwide.

Learn more about Dav at www.pilkey.com!
